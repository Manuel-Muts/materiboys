Facilities

Our School Facilities

Our school is designed to support learning, creativity, and healthy growth through engaging learning spaces and active student programs.

Computer Lab

Information Technology & Digital Learning

State-of-the-art computer lab equipped with modern workstations for coding, web development, and digital literacy training. Students engage in hands-on IT projects and industry-relevant skills development.

Science Laboratory

Biology, Chemistry & Physics

Well-equipped science labs provide students with practical experience in conducting experiments, observations, and scientific investigations. Fully stocked with modern laboratory equipment and safety features.

Library

Research & Knowledge Center

Comprehensive library with extensive collections of print and digital resources supporting academic research, independent study, and lifelong learning for all students.

Sports Facilities

Athletic Development & Wellness

Modern sports complex with fields, courts, and gymnasium facilities supporting diverse athletic programs including soccer, basketball, volleyball, rugby, and tennis.

Auditorium

Arts & Performances

Modern auditorium hosting school assemblies, theatrical productions, music performances, and cultural events. Complete with professional sound and lighting systems.

Dining Hall

Nutrition & Community Dining

Spacious dining facility serving nutritious meals prepared by professional kitchen staff. Comfortable seating areas promoting healthy eating habits and social interaction.
